Our Roots

A CENTURY
OF EXCELLENCE

The Department of Chemical Engineering at Jadavpur University stands as Asia's oldest, a titan established in 1921 under the pioneering vision of Prof. H.L. Roy. For over a century, it has forged engineers, scientists, and champions.

The Indian Institute of Chemical Engineers (IIChE) was founded on May 18, 1947 — days before Indian independence — with Jadavpur University at its heart. Our students' chapter carries that flame.

"Excellence is not a destination but a continuous journey that never ends."

Chemathalon is where the laboratory precision of chemical engineering meets the raw spirit of athletic competition. Every year, it unites minds and bodies in one magnificent festival of sport.

About the Event

WHAT IS
CHEMATHALON?

Chemathalon is the annual sports extravaganza of the IIChE Jadavpur University Students' Chapter. It is a celebration that transcends the classroom — a day where chemical engineers trade their lab coats for jerseys and compete with the same fire that drives their research.

Hosted on the historic grounds of Jadavpur University, Kolkata, the event spans multiple disciplines — from the football ground to the chess board — bringing together students from all years of the department in friendly but fierce competition.

It is more than a sports day. Chemathalon is a tradition, a community, a legacy — one that grows stronger with every passing year. Whether you're a seasoned athlete or a first-time player, the arena welcomes all.

"Where equations meet endurance. Where bonds are forged not just in reactions, but in rivalry and respect."
